NLB EduConnect 2024


Date
May 29, 2024
Event
On 29 May 2024, the Brain-NORM team was invited to join the NLB EduConnect 2024 event hosted at the Central Public Library. We shared useful classroom literacy tips and more about the Brain-NORM project with the early childhood educators in attendance. Visit the Clinical Brain Lab website ( https://www.clinicalbrain.org/post/brainnorm/) to find out more about the Brain-NORM project and other ongoing research projects! Stay tuned for future events!.
